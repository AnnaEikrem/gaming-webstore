    Description

    The Game Boy is a handheld gaming console released by Nintendo in 1989. It has an 8-bit processor, monochromatic screen, and uses cartridges for games. It became popular for its affordability, durability, and iconic games like Tetris and Super Mario Land. It paved the way for portable gaming and remains a nostalgic device among players.
